About Us

Muga Fish Farm Is The Best Fish Farm in the region

Muga Fish Farm was established in the year 2010 as a grow out farm but later in the year 2012, in partnership with FAO the farm was reorganized to be a fingerlings bulking or nursing farm with only 4 ponds. In the year 2013 the farm started producing her own fingerlings and has continued to do the fingerlings production and grow out. The farm has scaled up the production of fingerlings and to date the farm is among the largest fingerlings producers in Kenya Muga Fish Farm started as a sole proprietorship but in 2018 it was changed to a partnership company and registered as a partnership company under the name of Muga Aqua farms in the year 2020

Certified and Award Winning for the best innovative farmer and Service Provider

Muga fish farm is where innovation and imagination meets reality,we provide quality and exemplary services to our customers.Muga Fish Farm is an innovative aquaculture farm that comprises of nursing, brooding and grow-out sections for fish that incorporates an innovated hatchery. The farm was in 2021 voted to be the "Most Innovative and Sustainable Fish Farm" in Kenya. It aims at reducing fish scarcity through encouraging other farmers to venture in fish farming and enhance employment opportunities through fishing related enterprises in the community. The farm is a provider and generator of fish, fish fingerlings and fish products to the society and well as a role model in the fish farming industry.
